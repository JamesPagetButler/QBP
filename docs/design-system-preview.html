QBP Design System

"Brass orreries in a greenhouse"

A visual language bridging Victorian mechanical precision with organic, nature-inspired optimism. The aesthetic tells the story of QBP: moving from "broken theories" (industrial complexity) toward "working theories" (elegant natural truth).
